About

Hi, I'm Sarah and this is my research blog. I started this site in May 2022 as a way to document my journey in robotics and the knowledge I accumulate along the way.

To date, I've dealt mostly with the software side of robotics, involving path/mission planning and perception. I'm broadly interested in computer vision, autonomy, state estimation, and control. While new to low-level control, I find this sub-field of control to be particularly engrossing and highly entertaining. I mean, how does a dancing Spot not completely win you over? (Though to be fair, I think Spot's next routine needs more break dancing :] )

When I'm not destroying robots in simulation, you can probably find me playing classical guitar, weightlifting, or dirt biking.
